CLAIM
The ornamental design for an audio cable, as shown and described.
DESCRIPTION
FIG. 1 is a top, right, front perspective view of an audio cable showing the new design;
FIG. 2 is a front view thereof;
FIG. 3 is a rear view thereof;
FIG. 4 is a left side view thereof;
FIG. 5 is a right side view thereof;
FIG. 6 is a top view thereof;
FIG. 7 is a bottom view thereof;
FIG. 8 is an enlarged detail view thereof, corresponding to box 8 in FIG. 7;
FIG. 9 is a cross-sectional view thereof, taken along line 9-9 in FIG. 6; and,
FIG. 10 is a profile view thereof, corresponding to line 10-10 in FIG. 6.
The dashed lines shown in the drawings illustrate portions of the audio cable that form no part of the claimed design. The audio cable is shown with a symbolic break in its length. The appearance of any portion of the article between the break lines forms no part of the claimed design.
