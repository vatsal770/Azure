(57) ABSTRACT
The invention provides monoclonal antibodies that specifi- cally bind to TIGIT. The monoclonal antibodies have the capacity for substantial activation of T cells and natural killer cells by inhibiting binding of TIGIT to CD155. The monoclonal antibodies can be used for treatment of cancer and infectious disease, among other applications.
